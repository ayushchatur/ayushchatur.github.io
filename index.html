CI-CD Solutions

My work for performance harnessing via synthetic in house Mobile device and network emulation has helped save thousands of dollars for my previous organization and its open source contribution have also benefited community.

Big Data

Performant architecture and scalable solutions by intuitively leveraging big data technologies is a challenge I thrive for. In my work for LoKi realtime event driven pipeline levergaing Kafka as distributed event store and kafka stream, a cost factor of 16x reduction was achieved bolstered with capabilites of highly scalable fault tolerant architecture requiring just 3 servers to process 5 billion+ event daily.

HPC

My Current research extends focuses on Optimization of deep learning models for Chest CT-Scan on heterogenous platforms such as FPGA, GPU and Multicore CPUs. Leveraging MPI based gloo backend (PyTorch) for distributed model parallelism and employs architecture aware sparse optimization. With this work i aim to reduce the training time by a factor of 1.2 - 2x.
